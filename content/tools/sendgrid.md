# SendGrid

SendGrid (by Twilio) is one of the most established email delivery platforms. It handles both transactional email (password resets, receipts, notifications) and marketing email (campaigns, newsletters) at scale.

For agent workflows, SendGrid is a reliable choice when you need high-volume email delivery with strong deliverability. The API is well-documented and SDKs exist for most languages, making it easy to integrate as an agent tool.
